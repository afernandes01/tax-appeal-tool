How It Works

This tool automates the process of finding comparable properties for a Cook County property tax appeal. It supports two types of arguments:

Uniformity: Are similar properties in your neighborhood assessed at a lower rate per square foot? If so, your assessment may not be uniform.

Overvaluation: Does your assessment imply a fair market value higher than what comparable homes have actually sold for?

All data comes from the Cook County Open Data Portal. This tool currently supports single-family residential properties.
